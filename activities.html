Ìgbà-Dún Our Activities

Join us in celebrating and preserving Yorùbá culture through our diverse range of activities and programmes

Core Regular Activities

Language Learning Programme

  • Weekly Yorùbá language classes at different proficiency levels
  • Conversation practice groups
  • Digital learning resources and online sessions
  • Language exchange partnerships

Cultural Education Sessions

  • Weekly cultural workshops exploring Yorùbá traditions
  • Hands-on learning of traditional crafts
  • Cooking classes featuring Yorùbá cuisine
  • Traditional music and dance instruction

Community Support Initiatives

  • Welcome sessions for newcomers to Scotland
  • Mentoring programme pairing experienced members with newcomers
  • Regular social gatherings for cultural exchange
  • Intergenerational knowledge-sharing sessions

Arts and Performance Projects

  • Regular film screenings of Yorùbá cinema
  • Art exhibitions featuring local artists
  • Music performances and workshops
  • Theatre productions combining Scottish and Yorùbá influences

Annual and Special Events

Cultural Festival

Our annual celebration featuring performances, exhibitions, traditional cuisine, and community awards.

Educational Outreach

School visits, university partnerships, and cultural awareness training programmes.

Heritage Documentation

Recording and preserving stories of Yorùbá people in Scotland through various media.

Who Benefits

Youth and Young Adults

Access to cultural education, personal development opportunities, and peer support networks.

Yorùbá Community Members

Opportunities to maintain cultural connections and share knowledge with the wider community.

Scottish Institutions

Access to authentic cultural resources and enhanced ability to support diverse populations.
