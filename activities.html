Language Learning Programme

Our comprehensive language learning program is designed to make Yoruba language accessible to everyone, regardless of their starting point. Through structured classes, interactive sessions, and digital resources, we create an immersive learning environment.

Programme Benefits

  • Structured learning opportunities in Yoruba language
  • Regular opportunities for interaction and shared learning
  • Maintenance of cultural connections for Yoruba speakers
  • Cross-cultural communication and understanding
  • Support for newcomers' integration while maintaining cultural identity

Cultural Education Sessions

Our cultural education sessions go beyond surface-level appreciation to provide deep, authentic understanding of Yoruba traditions and practices.

Session Highlights

  • Hands-on learning experiences of Yoruba heritage
  • Intergenerational knowledge sharing
  • Traditional skills and practices preservation
  • Promotion of cultural authenticity
  • Building confidence in cultural identity and expression

Community Support Initiatives

Our community support programs create a strong network of connections and resources for all members.

Support Services

  • Regular group activities to combat social isolation
  • Sustainable support networks within the community
  • Navigation assistance for newcomers to Scottish society
  • Cultural exchange and shared experiences
  • Community cohesion through regular social interaction

Arts and Performance Projects

Through our arts and performance projects, we create platforms for cultural expression and collaboration.

Project Outcomes

  • Advancement of Yoruba arts in Scotland
  • Cultural representation in the public sphere
  • Collaborative projects between Scottish and Yoruba artists
  • Accessibility of Yoruba culture to wider audiences
  • Support for artists maintaining cultural art forms
