Cultural Festival

Our annual cultural festival is a celebration of Yoruba heritage, bringing together community members and the wider Scottish public for a vibrant showcase of culture, art, and tradition.

Festival Highlights

  • Traditional dance and music performances
  • Art exhibitions featuring local and international artists
  • Yoruba cuisine tastings and cooking demonstrations
  • Interactive cultural workshops
  • Community recognition awards

Educational Outreach

Our educational outreach programs bring Yoruba culture into Scottish schools and institutions, fostering cross-cultural understanding and appreciation.

Program Components

  • Interactive cultural presentations in schools
  • Academic collaborations with universities
  • Professional development for educators
  • Public lectures and seminars
  • Cultural resource development

Heritage Documentation

We are committed to preserving and documenting Yoruba cultural heritage in Scotland for future generations.

Documentation Projects

  • Recording oral histories from community elders
  • Creating digital archives of cultural practices
  • Documenting community events and celebrations
  • Publishing community stories and experiences
  • Building an accessible cultural database

Cultural Exchange Programme

Our exchange programs create meaningful connections between Scottish and Yoruba communities.

Exchange Activities

  • Organized visits to Yoruba-speaking regions
  • Virtual connections with cultural organizations
  • Collaborative projects with other cultural groups
  • Youth exchange opportunities
  • Cross-cultural artistic collaborations

Professional Development

We offer opportunities for community members to develop professional skills while preserving cultural practices.

Development Opportunities

  • Workshop series on cultural entrepreneurship
  • Traditional crafts skills development
  • Leadership training programs
  • Cultural consultation services training
  • Professional networking events

Get Involved
