Our Mission

Ìgbà-Dún is a Scottish charitable organisation dedicated to celebrating and sharing Yoruba culture while building stronger, more connected communities across Scotland. We combine cultural preservation with social impact, creating spaces where people can learn, connect, and grow together through engagement with Yoruba language, arts, and traditions.
